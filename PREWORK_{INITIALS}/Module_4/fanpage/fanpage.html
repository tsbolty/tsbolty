Why Queen was the best band of their generation

Queen was formed in 1970, consisting of four members:

  • Freddie Mercury
  • Brian May
  • Roger Taylor
  • John Deacon

They quickly released timeless hits we still know and love today such as:

  • Keep Yourself Alive
  • Seven Seas of Rhye
  • Bohemian Rhapsody
  • Under Pressure
  • Another One Bites The Dust
  • We Are The Champions
Unfortunately, tragedy struck when their lead singer, Freddie Mercury attracted the AIDS virus. This brought upon an abrupt end to the band in 1986. Luckily before they ended, they were able to perform at the largest concert ever concieved, Live Aid.
